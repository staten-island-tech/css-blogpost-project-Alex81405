Community Based Organizations I Am In

By: Alex Zhang

Thrive Scholars

The mission of Thrive Scholars is to help high-achieving, low-income underrepresented students get into and graduate from top colleges equipped to achieve their full career potential.

Matriculate

Matriculate is a FREE virtual college advising program that connects high-achieving low-income students with a near-peer college mentor who works one-on-one with students throughout the college and financial aid processes.

Chess in the Schools College Bound Program

Chess in the Schools College Bound Program is a four-year initiative for high school students, delivers college preparatory and job-readiness programming along with advanced chess instruction.
